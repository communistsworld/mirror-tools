<h1>red texts mirror</h1>
<p>This site mirrors the fantastic <a href="https://www.redtexts.org/"><em>red texts: communist text files</em> archive</a>,
and additionally hosts converted versions of the texts in the <code>.epub</code>, <code>.mobi</code> (Kindle) and <code>.pdf</code> formats.
All praise for collecting and formatting these texts goes to the admin of <em>red texts</em>. Check of his site for more information.</p>
